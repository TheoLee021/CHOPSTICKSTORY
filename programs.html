Our Programs

Discover the richness of Korean culture through our diverse educational programs.

MunDam: Infusing Culture

Explore traditional Korean folktales and contemporary narratives that showcase the wisdom and values of Korean culture.

Our MunDam program includes:

  • Traditional folktales and myths
  • Contemporary Korean literature
  • Storytelling workshops
  • Cultural context discussions

YeDam: Infusing Art

Experience hands-on learning with traditional Korean crafts. Yedam is a program where you can build patience, focus, and a sense of achievement through making and decorating.

Our YeDam program includes:

  • Chopstick making wood craft
  • Lacquering and mother-of-pearl inlay
  • Gyubang craft (traditional Korean sewing)

DeokDam: Infusing Virtue

Learn about the fundamental values and manners of Korean culture.

Our DeokDam program includes:

  • Table manners education
  • Tea culture education
  • Daily life manners

CheDam: Infusing Experiences

Engage in interactive sessions featuring classic Korean games that promote cultural understanding through play.

Our CheDam program includes:

  • Classic Korean games
  • Interactive sessions
  • Cultural context discussions
